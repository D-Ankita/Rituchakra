## Please read this carefully

RituChakra is an educational wellness app. It helps you observe your menstrual cycle and learn how classical Ayurveda and modern hormone biology describe the body's rhythm. It is **not a medical device, not a diagnostic tool, and not a substitute for a qualified healthcare professional.**

## 1. Not medical advice

All content within RituChakra — articles, rituals, herb descriptions, phase insights, AI-generated or human-authored text, practitioner quotes — is for **educational and informational purposes only**. It does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional (your physician, gynecologist, or a certified Ayurvedic practitioner — in India, a BAMS-qualified vaidya) for:
- Diagnosis of any condition.
- Treatment of any condition.
- Medication adjustments.
- Decisions about pregnancy, contraception, or fertility.
- Any symptom that is severe, persistent, worsening, or worrying.

## 2. Ayurvedic content — specific caveats

- **Traditional knowledge ≠ clinical prescription.** Recommendations derived from *Charaka Samhita*, *Sushruta Samhita*, *Ashtanga Hridaya*, and other classical texts represent 2,500+ years of lived wisdom. They are not clinical prescriptions in the modern allopathic sense.
- **Herbs and oils can interact with medications.** Turmeric, ginger, fenugreek, shatavari, ashoka, and many other Ayurvedic herbs can interact with blood thinners, diabetes medications, thyroid medication, and more. **Talk to a physician or qualified vaidya before adding any herb to your regimen, especially if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, trying to conceive, or on any medication.**
- **Contraindications exist.** Some rituals (e.g., intense abhyanga, nasya, certain detox protocols) are not appropriate during pregnancy, menstruation, acute illness, or certain medical conditions. Read ritual detail screens carefully before starting.
- **Not all practitioners agree.** Ayurvedic lineage traditions interpret the same text differently. Our content follows mainstream peer-reviewed interpretations and has been reviewed by a BAMS-qualified practitioner, but this does not make any single recommendation universally right for every body.

## 3. Cycle tracking — specific caveats

- **Not contraception.** Predicted ovulation windows and fertile days are statistical estimates, not guarantees. **Do not rely on RituChakra for contraception.** For reliable birth control, consult your doctor about hormonal, barrier, or IUD options.
- **Not a pregnancy test.** A late period can indicate pregnancy, stress, illness, weight change, travel, new medication, or dozens of other causes. Take a pregnancy test if there's any possibility.
- **Cycles vary.** If your cycle is irregular, absent, unusually heavy, unusually painful, or has changed suddenly — please see a doctor. These can be symptoms of PCOS, endometriosis, thyroid conditions, fibroids, or other issues that deserve proper medical evaluation.
- **Predictions get better over time.** Early predictions are educated guesses. They become more accurate as you log more cycles. Treat them as estimates, not promises.

## 4. When to seek medical attention immediately

Please consult a doctor (do not wait for the app to "figure it out") if you experience:
- Severe pelvic pain that is not relieved by usual pain medication
- Heavy bleeding that soaks through a pad or tampon every hour for several hours
- Bleeding between periods or after sex
- Sudden absent period in someone usually regular (when pregnancy is possible — take a test)
- Symptoms of ectopic pregnancy: sharp one-sided pain, shoulder pain, dizziness, faintness
- Signs of infection: fever, foul-smelling discharge, severe pain
- Any symptom that feels unusual or concerning to you

**In India, emergency helpline:** 102 (free emergency health service).

## 5. Mental health

Content about premenstrual mood, PMDD, luteal-phase low mood, and emotional cycles is provided for context and pattern-recognition. It is not a mental-health diagnostic tool.

If you experience persistent low mood, suicidal thoughts, or severe mood changes — please reach out to a mental health professional immediately.

**iCall (India) mental health helpline:** 9152987821 (Mon-Sat, 8 AM – 10 PM).
**Vandrevala Foundation helpline:** 1860-2662-345 (24/7).

## 8a. AI-generated responses (Rhythm chat)

RituChakra includes an AI chat feature called **Rhythm**, which answers your questions using our practitioner-reviewed article library and classical Ayurvedic texts as grounding.

**Important limitations:**

- AI responses are **not medical advice**. They are conversational summaries of our library content, filtered through a large language model.
- AI responses **may be inaccurate, incomplete, or miss nuance.** We use Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and multi-layer safety classifiers to minimize errors, but we cannot guarantee correctness of every response.
- The AI **does not know your full medical history**. It works with the cycle data you log and the conversation you have with it — nothing more.
- The AI **will not diagnose** conditions or prescribe dosages. It is designed to refuse those specific tasks.
- The AI **does not replace** your physician, Ayurvedic practitioner (BAMS-qualified vaidya), or mental health professional.

**Red-flag protocol:**

If you describe symptoms that our classifiers identify as potentially serious (severe bleeding, chest pain, suicidal ideation, etc.), the AI will interrupt the conversation and direct you to emergency services:
- **102** — free emergency health service (India)
- **iCall** — 9152987821 (mental health crisis)
- **Vandrevala Foundation** — 1860-2662-345 (24/7 mental health)

Please use those resources if the AI surfaces them. The AI is correctly stepping aside for you to get real help.

**Data handling for AI:**

- Your questions are sent to a third-party AI model provider (Anthropic / Claude or Google / Gemini — see Privacy Policy §6 for specifics).
- They retain inputs and outputs for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, then delete.
- Your questions are **not** used for AI model training unless you explicitly opt in under Settings → AI Privacy.
- You can delete your entire AI chat history at any time from Settings → AI Privacy → Delete history.

**If you feel the AI gave dangerous or incorrect advice:**

- Flag the response immediately (tap the flag icon on any AI message).
- Email **safety@rituchakra.app** with the chat ID.
- We review flagged responses weekly and adjust guardrails where needed.

By using the AI chat feature, you acknowledge these limitations and agree that the AI is a tool for conversation and reflection, not a source of medical authority.
