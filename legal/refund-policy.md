## Summary

- **App Store and Play Store purchases are refunded by them, not by us.** You must request refunds directly from Apple or Google.
- **We will always help you** request a refund or resolve a billing issue — email **support@rituchakra.app** and we'll act within 48 hours.
- **We do not issue refunds in cash.** All refunds flow through the original payment method via the store.

---

## 1. What you're buying

A RituChakra subscription gives you access to Premium features (full Ayurveda library, daily ritual recommendations, rhythm insights, extended tracking, PDF exports) for the duration of your billing period. When you subscribe:

- Your payment is processed by Apple (if you subscribed on iOS) or Google (if on Android).
- Your subscription auto-renews at the end of each period unless cancelled.
- You can cancel anytime from your App Store / Play Store settings or in-app (Settings → Subscription).

## 2. How to cancel

- **iOS:** Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → RituChakra → Cancel
- **Android:** Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → RituChakra → Cancel
- **In-app shortcut:** Settings → Subscription → Cancel (this deep-links you to the store)

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You keep Premium access until that date; you are **not refunded pro-rata for the unused portion** automatically — see §4 for when refunds do apply.

## 3. Free trial

- A 7-day free trial is offered on the annual plan for new subscribers only.
- You can cancel the trial anytime before it ends — you will not be charged.
- We will send a reminder email + in-app notification 48 hours before the trial ends.
- If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your card/UPI will be charged for the full annual amount automatically. This is a requirement of Apple / Google's billing systems.

## 4. When refunds apply

### 4.1 Accidental or unwanted charges
If you were charged in error (e.g., you thought you cancelled but didn't), request a refund from Apple or Google within **14 days of the charge**. They typically grant these.
- Apple: [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
- Google: [play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory](https://play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory)

### 4.2 Technical issues preventing use
If a bug, outage, or technical failure prevents you from using the Service you paid for, and we are unable to resolve it promptly:
- Contact **support@rituchakra.app** with your order ID and a description of the issue.
- We will coordinate with Apple or Google to request a refund on your behalf.
- Resolution timeline: up to 7 working days.

### 4.3 Duplicate charges
If you were charged twice for the same period (e.g., you accidentally subscribed on both iOS and Android), contact **support@rituchakra.app** — we will refund the duplicate via the appropriate store.

### 4.4 Subscription change
If you switch from monthly to annual (or vice versa) mid-cycle, Apple / Google prorate automatically. No manual refund is needed.

## 5. When refunds do NOT apply

- **Change of mind** after the free trial expires and the paid period begins (beyond the store's standard refund window). However, you may cancel to prevent future renewal charges.
- **Unused portion** of a subscription period. Cancellation stops future renewals; you retain access for the period you paid for.
- **Dissatisfaction with content** — we offer a generous 7-day trial specifically for this reason. Please use it.
- **Downgrading** from Premium to Free mid-period.

## 6. Indian consumer law protections

Nothing in this policy limits rights you have under:
- **Consumer Protection Act, 2019** — covers unfair trade practices, defective services, misleading advertisements.
- **Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020** — covers transparent disclosure of recurring charges, easy cancellation, fair dealings.

If you believe you've been wronged and we can't resolve it, you may:
- File a complaint with the National Consumer Helpline: [consumerhelpline.gov.in](https://consumerhelpline.gov.in) or 1915
- Approach the relevant Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (district / state / national) based on your claim amount.

## 7. How long refunds take

- **Apple:** typically 3–5 business days to reflect on your payment method
- **Google Play:** typically 1–4 business days
- **UPI:** usually same-day to 48 hours

## 8. Grievance redressal

If you are unhappy with our refund handling:
1. Email **grievance@rituchakra.app** with your order details and what went wrong.
2. We will acknowledge within 48 hours and resolve within 30 days (per Consumer Protection Rules, 2020).
3. If unresolved, you may approach the Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission as above.
