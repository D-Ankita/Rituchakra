# Classical Ayurvedic Source Texts

**Status:** content sourcing research

**Brihat Trayi** (the great three foundational texts):
- *Charaka Samhita* — P.V. Sharma trans., Chaukhamba Orientalia, 1981
- *Sushruta Samhita* — K.L. Bhishagratna trans., Chaukhamba Orientalia, 1991 (3 vols)
- *Ashtanga Hridaya* — Shri Kanta Murthy trans., Chaukhamba Orientalia, 1991 (3 vols)

**Secondary integrative sources:**
- Claudia Welch — *Balance Your Hormones, Balance Your Life*
- Robert Svoboda — *Ayurveda for Women: A Guide to Vitality and Health*
- Maya Tiwari — *Women's Power to Heal Through Inner Medicine*
- Dr. Vasant Lad — *The Complete Book of Ayurvedic Home Remedies*
