# DPDP Act 2023 · Health App Compliance

**Status:** research note

India's first comprehensive data protection law. Consent-based processing (§6). Data minimization. Mumbai region required for data residency. Data Principal rights: access, correction, erasure, nomination, grievance redressal (§11-14). Grievance officer mandatory. Max penalty ₹250 crore. Full compliance deadline **May 13, 2027**. Privacy policy must be in plain English + optionally one of the 22 Eighth Schedule languages.
