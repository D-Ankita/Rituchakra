# Flo Health — Competitor Analysis

**Status:** research note · drafted during market exploration phase

Flo Premium global pricing $9.99/mo · $49.99/yr. The "Pass It On Project" offers free Premium to women in India (Android only) — worth noting as a competitive tactic. Strong dataset, AI predictions, community features. Weak on cultural framing for Indian users. No Ayurveda angle. Clinically competent but emotionally generic.
