# RituChakra

### The rhythm of seasons within you.

RituChakra is a privacy-first period and cycle tracking app that helps you understand your body's natural rhythm — without ever sending your data to the cloud. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Just you and your cycle.

---

## Why RituChakra?

Most period trackers ask you to trade your most intimate health data for basic features. RituChakra takes a different approach:

- **Your data never leaves your phone.** Everything is stored locally on your device. No servers, no syncing, no third parties.
- **No account required.** Open the app and start tracking in under a minute.
- **Wellness-first language.** We talk about phases, energy, and patterns — not clinical jargon.

---

## Features

### Intelligent Period Predictions

RituChakra learns from your unique patterns. The more you track, the smarter it gets.

- Predicts your next period with a confidence score (low, moderate, high)
- Adapts predictions as it learns your cycle over time
- Supports cycle lengths from 21 to 40 days
- Switch to manual mode anytime if you prefer to set your own cycle length

### Phase-Aware Daily Insights

Know what's happening in your body today — not just when your period is coming.

- See your current cycle phase (Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulation, Luteal) at a glance
- Get daily wellness tips tailored to your phase: what to eat, how to move, and how to rest
- Understand the "why" behind how you feel on any given day

### Comprehensive Daily Logging

Track as much or as little as you want. Every day is different.

- **Flow:** None, Light, Medium, or Heavy
- **12 Symptoms:** Cramps, headache, bloating, acne, fatigue, back pain, breast tenderness, nausea, dizziness, mood swings, cravings, insomnia
- **8 Moods:** Calm, happy, anxious, irritable, sad, energetic, sensitive, focused
- **Energy & Sleep:** Rate your energy (1–5), sleep quality (1–5), and log sleep hours
- **Medications & Notes:** Keep a record of what you took and how you felt in your own words

### Beautiful Calendar View

Your cycle, mapped out visually.

- Month view with phase-colored backgrounds so you can see your cycle at a glance
- Tap any day to view or add a log
- Quick "Mark Period" mode to rapidly log multiple period days
- Today is always highlighted so you never lose your place

### Hormone & Energy Visualizations

Understand the invisible patterns driving your cycle.

- See estimated estrogen, progesterone, and energy curves throughout your cycle
- Beautiful gradient charts that make complex biology approachable
- A current-day marker shows exactly where you are in the cycle
- Educational — helps you connect how you feel to what's happening hormonally

### Pattern Recognition & History

The longer you track, the more you learn about yourself.

- **Symptom Frequency:** See your top symptoms ranked by how often they appear
- **Cycle History:** Review past cycles with start dates and lengths
- **Personal Insights:** After 3 completed cycles, unlock personalized pattern analysis unique to your body

### Gentle Onboarding

Get started in two simple steps:

1. When did your last period start?
2. How long is your typical cycle?

Don't know? That's okay — tap "I'm not sure" and the app uses healthy defaults. You can always adjust later.
